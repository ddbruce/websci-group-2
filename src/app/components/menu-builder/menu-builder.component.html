Directions

Welcome to the menu-builder, where you can build your menu at your fingertips! Right now, designing changes aren't saved in our database but we will eventually add the ability to add customizable changes!

Add Section - Create a new section box.

Add Spacer - Add a box with no content that is the same color as your background - used for spacing out the sections.

Save Menu - Save additions of your menu to the database.

Drag and resize your sections and spacers however you like!

Right-click a section to edit its contents.
